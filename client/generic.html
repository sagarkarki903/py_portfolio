My Passions & Interests

There's more to me than code. Here's a glimpse into my creative and community side.

Outside the world of technology, I’m deeply passionate about **arts and music**. I love expressing myself through different creative outlets — from **playing the guitar** and **singing**, to **drawing** in my free time. Music and art have always been a grounding part of my life, helping me stay inspired and balanced.

I also believe in giving back and being involved in communities. I currently serve as the **Senior Representative** of the Nepalese Student Association at ULM, where we celebrate culture and support fellow students from Nepal. I'm a proud member of **Upsilon Pi Epsilon**, the international honor society for the computing and information disciplines, selected based on academic excellence.

Back in Nepal, I was a member of the **Rotaract Club**, actively volunteering and participating in social work initiatives. At ULM, I also engage with fellow tech enthusiasts through **ACM (Association for Computing Machinery)**, where we explore and discuss cutting-edge topics in computer science.

Whether it's tech, creativity, or community — I enjoy diving deep, learning continuously, and growing in all aspects of life.
